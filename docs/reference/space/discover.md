# Discover

![Discover](./assets/discover/auto.png)

The **Discover** page is designed to facilitate collaboration and alliance building among stakeholders.

## Purpose

This section allows you to find published commitments that are looking for partners for joint submission. It is an essential tool for networking and amplifying the impact of your pledges by joining forces with other organizations.

## Finding Commitments

* **Filtering & Searching:** You can filter the list of commitments by region or topics, or use specific keywords to search for pledges that align with your organization's goals.
* **Visibility:** Only commitments that have been published and explicitly marked as **joint commitments** by their creators will appear in this list.

## Connecting with Pledgers

When you find a commitment of interest:

1. Click the **Notify Pledger** button associated with the commitment.
2. This action sends a notification to the original pledger, initiating a potential collaboration.
3. The notification includes your name, email address, and relevant details about your organization, allowing the pledger to review your profile and start a conversation.

## List of Submitted Joint Commitments

The main area of the page displays a table of these joint commitments, providing a quick overview of available partnerships.
