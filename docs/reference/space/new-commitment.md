# New Commitment

![New Commitment](./assets/new-commitment/full-auto.png)

The **New Commitment** form is where you provide the details of your organization's pledge to the Global Disability Summit.

## Form Structure

The form is divided into several logical sections to capture all necessary information. Fields marked with an asterisk (*) are mandatory.

### General Information

*   **Title of Commitment:** A concise title for your pledge (maximum 150 characters).
*   **Text of Commitment:** A rich text editor where you describe the commitment in detail. A "Preview" tab is available to review the formatting.

### Geographical Scope and Location

*   **Implementation Scope:** A dropdown to specify the level of implementation (e.g., Global, Regional, National).
*   **Continent:** A dropdown to select the primary continent where the commitment will be implemented.

### Categorization

*   **Topic:** Select the specific topic that best describes the focus of your commitment.
*   **Pillars:** Checkboxes to indicate which GDS pillars your commitment falls under. You can select multiple pillars, such as:
    *   Non-discrimination, Equality & Participation
    *   Accessibility
    *   Situations of crisis and conflict
    *   Social and economic rights

### Timeframe

*   **Timeframe:** A dropdown to specify the expected duration or completion date for the commitment.

## Submission

Once all required fields are filled, you can click the **Create new Commitment** button to save your pledge. If you intend to make multiple pledges, you must complete this form separately for each individual commitment.
