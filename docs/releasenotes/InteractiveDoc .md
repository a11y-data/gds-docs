## Welcome tab

Click here for the entry point of the application.

## Discover tab

Here you can find a list of all the available commitments that are looking for partners. You can search for a specific commitment by typing in the search bar. You can also filter the commitments by category.

You can click on a commitment to see more details and to express your interest in joining it.

## Commitments tab

This is where you can see and manage all the commitments you have created. This would also be the place where you can create new commitments.

Commitments that you have opened for editing will be displayed here. You can click on a commitment to see more details and to edit it.

You need to be signed in to access content for this tab.

## Reporting tab

A place for reporting on the progress of your commitments. You can see the progress of your commitments and download reports.

You need to be signed in to access content for this tab.

## Messages tab

This page displays all the messages you have received from the GDS secretariat.

You need to be signed in to access content for this tab.

## Organisation tab

Here you can see and manage your organisation's profile like the name, logo, and contact information.

You need to be signed in to access content for this tab.

## Team tab

This is where you can see and manage the members of your organisation. You can add new members, remove existing ones, and assign roles.

You need to be signed in to access content for this tab.

## Welcome page

This is the page you are currently on. It is the entry point of the application.

It provides explanations about the purpose of the application and the main features.

It also allow easy access to:

- `Report On Progress` to report on the progress of your commitments (only active when the reporting is open)
- `Commit New Pledges` to create a new commitments
- `Edit Organisation` to edit your organisation's profile

## Welcome page (not signed in)

This is the page you are currently on. It is the entry point of the application.

It provides explanations about the purpose of the application and the main features.

When you are not signed-in (as it is the case now), you can start the registration process by clicking on the `Start Submitting Commitments` button.

## Discover - list

This list displays all the commitments that are looking for partners. You can:

- look for a specific commitment by typing in the search bar. You can also filter the commitments by category, pillar, topics or type of organisation;
- click on a row to see more details and to express your interest in joining it;
- notify pledgers to start a discussion about a commitment by clicking on the `Notify Pledger` button.

## Commitment - Joint Commitment

Clicking here will take you to the page for creating a new joint commitment.

Joint commitments are commitments that are created by two or more organisations working together, each contributing to the commitment.

Submitting a joint commitment will allow you to find partners to work with you on a commitment.

## Commitment - Network with partners

This will direct you to a page where you can see all the commitments that are looking for partners. You can search for a specific commitment by typing in the search bar. You can also filter the commitments by category.

## Commitment - new

Click here to create a new commitment.

## Commitment - list

This list displays all the commitments you have created. You can:

- click on a row to see more details and to edit it;
- filter commitments any of the fields in the grid header.

## Commitment - action bar

This is the action bar that displays all the actions you can perform on a commitment in a given time.

Actions include:

- `Edit` to toggle the commitment to edit mode;
- `Print` to display the commitment in a printable format (PDF);
- `Delete` to delete the commitment;
- `Save` to save your changes;
- `Cancel` to cancel your changes;
- `Submit` to submit the commitment for approval. A commitment can only be submitted when all required fields are filled-in.

## Commitment - content

This is the content of the commitment. You can edit the content by clicking on the `Edit` button.

## Commitment - create

Click here to create a new commitment.

## Commitment - categorization

This is where you can categorize your commitment. You can select the category, pillar, and topics.

## Commitment - location

This is where you can specify the location of your commitment. You can select the country and the region and the level of the implementation scope.

## Commitment - context

This will help understand the problem you are trying to solve with your commitment.

## Commitment - group

Tell us how specific groups are involved in your commitment, and how you address non-discrimination and gender equality.

## Commitment - partnership

This is where you can specify the partners you are looking for and what will be your own contribution to the commitment.
